Operational Dashboard

The VitalSchedule operational dashboard provides staff with real-time risk assessment and actionable intervention recommendations.

Dashboard Overview

The Overview tab shows appointments at risk today, with an improvement summary showing expected vs. predicted no-shows with VitalSchedule interventions.

Today's Appointments

The Appointments tab provides detailed risk scores for each patient, with personalized intervention recommendations based on specific risk factors.

Intervention Tracking

The Interventions tab tracks completed interventions and their outcomes, with effectiveness rates for different intervention types.

Impact Analysis

The Impact tab shows concrete results by department, with monthly metrics on appointments saved, revenue recovered, and provider time saved.
